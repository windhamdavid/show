What are Google Apps ™ ?

They are a suite of web applications made available as cloud hosted software as a service that can be administered for a group of users at a particular domain name.

I have nine active google accounts. I’m support the products. Solid, secure, easy to use, well documented.

AdSense, Alerts, Analytics, Blogger, Books, Calendar, Chrome Sync, Contacts, Custom Search, Docs, FeedBurner, Finance, Friend Connect, Fusion Tables, Gmail, Google+, Groups, Location History, Maps, Merchant Center, Moderator, News, Orkut, Picasa, Play Music, Profile, Sites, Talk, Task, Voice, Wallet / Checkout, Web History, Webmaster Tools, & YouTube
